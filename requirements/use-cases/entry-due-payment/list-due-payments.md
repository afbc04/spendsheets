# Summary

- **Title:** List Due Payment Entries
- **Description:** The system gives a list of the due payments undone registered
- **Pre-condition:** -
- **Pos-condition:** Due Payment Entries undone are listed

# Flow

### Normal Flow

1. User indicates he wants to list due payment entries
2. System list due payment entries, providing informations such as:
    - ID
    - ID of Category
    - Date of last change
    - Due Date
    - Days until due date
    - Status
    - Money need to be paid or to receive
